Signing Up

Instructions

To use our services, you need to register with us. In order to register with us, please click on the "Sign up" button and enter your details.


Creating Teams

Instructions


After signing in, you can create teams using "Create Team" buttons as shown below.



Just enter the name of the team you want to create and click on "Create Team" button

Sending Invitations

Instructions


You can invite team members to attend the birthday celebration events.

To send an invitation, click on "Send Invitation" button

  • First you need to select your team from the dropdown.

  • Next select the member whose birthday are you celebrating.

  • Select the date the time when you will we celebrating the event.

  • Next enter the location of the event.

  • And finally, any message that you want to sent to the team members.

Sending Greetings

Instructions


You can send greeting cards to the team members on their birthdays and on other special events.

To send a greeting card, click on "Send Greeting" button


  • First you need to select your team from the dropdown.

  • Next select the member whom you want to sent the greeting card.

  • Next write the message you want to sent to the member.

  • Click on the "Preview Card" button to preview the card before sending it.
